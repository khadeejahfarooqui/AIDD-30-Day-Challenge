SPECKit Plus is a simple and clear system that helps you make any project in a proper way.It tells you how to start a project, how to write details, how to plan everything, and then how to build it step by step.It also saves your thinking and your decisions, so next time you can work faster and better.With SPECKit Plus, every project becomes easy because everything is organized.

*‚≠ê 2. Short Answers for the 5 Core Concepts*
1) /constitution
This step tells the main purpose of the project.
Here you write what the project is about, why you are making it, who will use it, and what are the limits.It gives your project a clear direction from the start.

2) /specify 
In this step you write everything clearly about what you want to build.
You explain the feature, what it should do, what users want, and how the result should look.So there is no confusion later.

3) /plans
This is where you make a full plan of how you will build the feature.
You decide the steps, the order, and which tools or tech you will use.It makes the whole work easy to follow.

4) /tasks
In this step, you break your plan into small and simple tasks.
Each task is easy to understand and easy to do.It helps you complete the project step by step.

5) /implement
This is the building step.
Here you follow the tasks and start making the real feature, writing the code, and putting everything together.
This step turns your plan into a working project.
